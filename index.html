The McGuire-Hartman Estate

Built in 1957

The McGuire-Hartman Estate was built in 1957 by Donald William Hartman. Donald farmed the land and raised miniture ponies, cows and poultry. Donald proudly served in the US Army during world war II. He was an avid Tigers fan. Along with Donald's love for the land he also collected antique tractors. Interesting fact: The McGuire-Hartman Estate has had a consistent male occupancy only bearing the name Donald. Donald Hartman sr., Donald Hartman Jr., Donald McGuire Sr. and Donald McGuire Jr.


The McGuire-Hartman Estste was built in 1957, featuring 10 rooms, a 3 car garage , 3 out buildings, and is currently nestled on 3 carefully manicured acres. Fortified by the Brick construction, this 2800 square foot ranch estate has 10 rooms and a spacious 2700 square foot basement.In 1980 horse stables were added to the splender of this amazing estate.In the summer of 2017 a chicken coop on the grounds was converted to a charming schoolhouse for the McGuire children.
